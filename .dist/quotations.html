Quotations and citation

Blockquote for Quotations

the blockquote element defines a section from another source

For 50 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by 1.2 million members in the United States and close to 5 million globally.

q for short quotations

the q tag defines a short quotation: Browsers normally insert quotation marks around the quotation.

abbr for abbreviations

abbr tag defines an abbreviation or an acronym, like WHO

cite for work title

cite tag defines the title of a creative work (e.g. a book, a poem, a song, a movie, a painting, a sculpture, etc.). The Scream

tag is used to override the current text direction: This text will be written from right to left
